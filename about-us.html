You are in safe hands

With over 30 years experience in the industry, here at Amaya Sales UK we understand the importance of having the right garment decoration equipment for your business. We aim to help you every step of the way, whether you are a small start-up business or large established business, we will provide your company with the right product and support to help your business flourish.

Amaya Sales UK Ltd. specialise in the supply of direct to garment printers, embroidery machines, transfer paper, garment vinyl and so much more. We are proud to have long standing close relationships with with our manufacturers.
